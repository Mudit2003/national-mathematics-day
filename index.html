National Mathematics Day Celebration

Commemorating the Legacy of Srinivasa Ramanujan

Date: 22nd December, 2023

Join us in commemorating the birth anniversary of India’s famed mathematician, Srinivasa Ramanujan. This day serves to reflect on the significance of mathematics in our lives and the development of humanity.

Event Overview

Venue: Dera Natung Govt. College, Itanagar
Organized by: Dera Natung Govt. College in collaboration with APSCS&T

Competitions Overview & Terms

Essay Writing Competition

  • Theme: "Mathematics Unites"
  • Word Limit: 1000 words
  • Language: English or Hindi
  • Time: One hour

Drawing Competition

  • Theme: Influence of Mathematics in Society
  • Materials: Participants bring their own
  • Time: One hour

Slogan Writing Competition

  • Theme: Popularizing Mathematics
  • Word Limit: 50 words
  • Language: English or Hindi
  • Medium: Any form of art on a plain sheet

Story-telling Competition

  • Theme: Mathematics in Everyday Life
  • Time: 5 mins per participant
  • Medium: English or Hindi

Quiz Competition

  • Rounds: Four rounds of questions
  • Teams: Two participants per team
  • Medium: English or Hindi
